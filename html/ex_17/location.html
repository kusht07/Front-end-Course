Direction from Winnipeg to Gimli

Time and Kilometers: 1 hr 15 mins and 97 kms

  1. Head west on Riviera Ave toward Pembina Hwy/Rte 42 N
  2. Turn right onto Pembina Hwy/Rte 42 N
  3. Keep right to stay on Pembina Hwy/Rte 42 N, follow signs for Rte 62 N
  4. Continue straight onto Donald St/Rte 42 N
  5. Turn right onto Portage Ave/Rte 57 E/Rte 85 W (signs for Route 57 E/Route 85 E)
  6. Use the left 2 lanes to turn left onto Main St/Rte 52 N (signs for Route 57 E)
  7. Continue straight onto Main St/Rte 42/Rte 52 N
  8. Use the right lane to take the MB-101 W/Perimeter Highway ramp
  9. Merge onto MB-101 W
  10. Take exit 69 for MB-8 N toward Gimli
  11. Keep right at the fork, follow signs for MB-8 N/Gimli
  12. Turn right onto Gimli Park Rd
  13. We are located on the main beach in Gimli just north of the main dock and Lakeview Resort Hotel
